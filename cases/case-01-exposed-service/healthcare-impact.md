# Healthcare Impact – Insecure Remote Access

## Scenario
If a similar misconfiguration existed in a hospital environment:

## Potential Impact
- Unauthorized access to clinical systems
- Exposure of patient data
- Manipulation of medical systems or configurations

## Clinical Risk
- Incorrect data may lead to misdiagnosis
- Delayed or inappropriate treatment
- Loss of trust in healthcare IT systems

## Detection
- Monitoring unusual remote connections
- Logging and auditing access attempts
- Network traffic analysis for unauthorized protocols

## Prevention
- Disable legacy protocols (e.g., Telnet)
- Enforce secure access methods (SSH)
- Implement network segmentation
- Apply strict authentication policies
